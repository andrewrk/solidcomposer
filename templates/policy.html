Policy

Your Rights

SolidComposer does not claim any rights over anything you upload. It's all yours - we won't touch anything that intellectually belongs to you. We will take measures to make sure that any work you create that is private (e.g. in your project manager and not in a competition) is protected from public access.

Our Rights

Don't sue us! I don't have the money or the heart to fight it. For any publicly viewable songs, we have the right to remove anything we feel like for any reason. Stuff in your own project manager we won't even look at, except of course the bandwidth and file size load you put on the server.

Rules

  1. Don't be a jerk.
  2. Constructive criticism.
  3. There is a special circle of Hell reserved for spammers and song-thieves.
