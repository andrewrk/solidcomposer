What's New

"Boten Anna" Release

September 1st, 2010

Thanks to JIIP for the new logo!

The biggest new thing is the redesign and the Workbench, of course. On top of that, SolidComposer is now on a more powerful server.

New Features

  • Workbench - Seriously, go check it out!
  • Song Comments - You can now freely reply to any comment, as well as timed song comments.
  • /me actions in chat - So now you can virtually slap people with a large trout.
  • Hyperlink support in chat
  • Tab-completion for usernames in chat - Start typing the beginning of someone's name and the rest will auto-fill.
  • Page up/down to scroll in chat - the less mouse, the better.
  • Markdown support - You can use markdown to format comments, rules, themes, etc.
  • Gravatar support - You can change your globally recognized avatar on gravatar.com
  • UserVoice Feedback Forum - voice your opinion. This forum is for bug reports, feature requests, and suggestions.
  • Password Recovery - making remembering passwords obsolete since 1999.
  • Change Password - you can change your password now.
  • Email Subscription Settings - SolidComposer will be sending more mail now. Change your settings.

Bug Fixes for Arena

  • Chat bugs are fixed. - this includes double post, linebreak shenanigans, and general slowness.
  • Entry list looks better with long song names.
  • Entry list and chat fit side by side on 1024px monitors.
